Drills & Techniques

Batting Drills

  • Front Foot Drive Drill: Improves timing and balance for front-foot shots.
  • Pull Shot Drill: Enhances reaction time and execution of pull shots.
  • Defense Drill: Helps in developing a solid defensive technique against fast bowlers.
  • Throw Down Drill: Refines shot selection and stroke play using throwdowns.
  • One-Handed Batting Drill: Strengthens control and wrist movement for better shot accuracy.

Bowling Drills

  • Target Bowling Drill: Enhances accuracy by aiming at specific spots on the pitch.
  • Run-up Drill: Focuses on perfecting a smooth and consistent bowling run-up.
  • Yorker Bowling Drill: Improves the ability to deliver yorkers consistently.
  • Wrist Position Drill: Develops better wrist control for swing and seam bowling.
  • Variation Drill: Helps bowlers practice different types of deliveries like slower balls and cutters.
